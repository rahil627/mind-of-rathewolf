9/19/13 in Japan
I was at my height of senses in Hong Kong because I was transitioning from a developing county to a developed one. That is how I should think all of the time. But then, if I did, I wouldn't have friends. It's tough to live in that state of mind. One has to step down.

Absurdity is hit often when I go from a developed country to a developing country, and vice versa. For some reason, my brain takes a lot longer to make sense of society, the role of technology, and just what the heck people are doing.
